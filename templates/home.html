Heyy Buddy!

Welcome to our platform Budget Buddy It is a platform/website where user can save data of their current incomes & expenses and keep track of the current financial status in regular intervals with generated stats. We provide user-friendly interface where user can access with an ease. Just scroll down and finish Quick tour about our platform.

Signup

Click Get Started button (at top-right of homepage) you will be redirected to Signup page where you need to register for an Budget Buddy account.

Username must be unique and it will give an alert if you're using emailid which is already existing etc.. if all the fields enterd are valid. you will get Congratulations! text within same page.

Login

After Successfully...! registering your account Click on Sign in you will be redirected to login page. Enter your Username and Password click Login.you will be landing in your personal Dashboard consisting of your recent financial stats.

Note: If you forget Username or Password Click on Forgot Username/Password. Enter your Email id and submit, credentials will be sent to your mail.

Dashboard

Your personal Dashboard is ready. If you are new to our platform, Dashboard values are set to '0'

Basically, dashboard is to show your recent month stats.

Set monthly limit

First, you need to set monthly limit to record your daily transactions. Select month & Enter value, Click submit.You cannot overwrite monthlylimit.

Note: If you want to update monthly limit click on Click here to Update limit

Add Budget

Select date, Choose '+' if it's income and '-' if it's an expense, Add Description of the transaction, Enter the value, Click on Add All the Income, Expense related transactions of that particular date will be displayed.

Note: You must set limit for particular month before adding the budget.

Budget History

The transactions history of any specific date will be accessed using Manage Budget

Select date and click on submit, All transactions of that particular date will be displayed

Note: Click remove on particular transaction to delete that transaction data

Change password

It's Optional. To change your account password

Enter your Current password, Enter your New password twice for confirmation

Logout

Finally..! after all, you need to Logout account

Note: If you fail to logout, you will be automatically loggedout when you close your browser
